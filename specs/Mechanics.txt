-----COMBAT------

Combat is primarily based on a throw two D20.
The target value is computed by:

21 - (weapon_lvl-player_lvl)

if the attacker rolls between 35 and 40 the target can't defend
and an additional 50% damage is dealt.

the atackee does a defense roll with two D20 with 
a target value of:

21 - (weapon_lvl-player_lvl)
